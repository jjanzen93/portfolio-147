Experiment 1 - brat generator

Description

The album "brat" and its several offshoots have a certain style to their names. I seek to replicate this.

Technical

I simply provided a list of nouns, adjectives, and addendums to string those adjectives together. The font and color are chosen to look like the original "brat" album cover.

Reflection

This ended up being a lot funnier than I initally thought. My favorite generation was "the united states government but it's not in a new economic era of progress so it's a significant downgrade and it's worse".
