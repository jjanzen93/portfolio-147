Experiment 2 - Image Generator

Description

This project seeks to replicate the nostalgic carpets found in arcades from arcades of (relative) old.

Technical

I used the random seed to determine colors (from a selected list), shape orientation, location, and the amount. The stars were drawn using an example function from the p5.js documentation. The shapes respond to the mouse location using a helper function, which returns a map that takes in an upper and lower bound on scale size, as well as an interpretation of distance between the shapes and the cursor.

Reflection

Visual design is far and away my weakest area of design, both in enjoyment and skill. As such, this provided a significant challenge for me and took me far out of my comfort zone. I do feel like I did this project to the best of my ability, and connected what we were meant to do with an interesting and creative example to replicate.
